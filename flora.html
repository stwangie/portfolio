Background

Midfulness meditation has shown positive effects on health, emotion, and cognition, such as improve attentional and emotional self-regulation. Nowadays, with thousands of new meditation app on the market, people can easily gain access to guided meditation and learn how to practice. Due to the effect of meditation,however, practitioners need to take a long time to gain psychological and physical feedback. Many of them have trouble establishing regular daily practice. The issue of how to giving beginners sufficient feedback and support to form a habit of meditation becomes critical. Therefore, we decided to design and prototype a meditation app that motivates people to meditate consistently.





Competitive Analysis

To identify the gaps and opportunities in the market, we conducted a competitive analysis, selecting five meditation apps as direct competitors and two self-care apps as indirect competitors which include meditation function as well. We analyzed features of customization, content, social support, and tracking progress.
