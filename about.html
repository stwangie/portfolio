Ssu-Ting Wang

#Psychology #UserExperience #AnimalAdvocate

I am currently a graduate student at iSchool at the University of Texas at Austin, focusing on UX research and design.


Majoring Psychology in college, I immersed myself in studying human’s mind and behavior. With four years of psychology studies, I built solid research skills, including experimental design, survey design, interview, and data analysis. Most importantly, it equipped me with strong empathy to comprehend people’s cognition, affects and behaviors.


With one year experiences as an account manager in media agency, I found my strong passion for exploring people's needs and creating enjoyable experiences for users. Now, I am honing my UX research and design skills with an effort to synthesize what I learned from psychology, what I experienced in digital marketing, and what I observed in my daily life.
