Overview

QEESI (Quick Environmental Exposure and Sensitivity Inventory)is a validated questionnaire for chemical intolerance and the most widely used screening instrument available for personal use. From the client kickoff, we learned that our client wanted to ensure the QEESI is usable by the general population and understand hoe to make it shareable with doctors. Our goal is to identify issues users may have from taking the QEESI to sharing results with doctors, as well as provide our client with feasible suggestions by conducting heuristic evaluation, competitive analysis, and usability testing.
